About

Keith Dumont is a Licensed Occupational Therapist and Registered Yoga Instructor. Keith received his Yoga Teaching Certification from Pure Yoga in New York City and spent six months in Hawaii studying Classical Hatha under his primary teacher, Yogi Charu. Since then, he has taught in New York City, San Antonio, California, Hawaii, Seattle, and currently resides in Venice, CA.
